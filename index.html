/ 04 About the operator

One person. Eight years on the web. Zero outsourcing.

When you hire IntegrityBuilds you're not hiring an agency that subcontracts to someone in another time zone. You're hiring me, directly. The same person reads your brief, writes the code, and answers your messages.

I'm Stephen. Based in Maryland, USAF veteran, former intelligence analyst. Building websites started as a kid's hobby. I started with templates and outgrew them.

The intel work taught me to read a brief carefully, ask the right questions, and deliver something that actually answers them. Eight years of building on my own taught me the rest: how to make a site load fast, how to balance how it looks with how it works, and how to handle the unglamorous parts like security, SEO, and deploys without breaking anything.

IntegrityBuilds is the studio version of that. Every site is custom-coded for the client. No themes, no page builders, no template reskins. If it ships under my name, I wrote it.
